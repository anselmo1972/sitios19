Purpose of the project

          

The purpose of the project is for us to learn how to make a web page for different              types of people But we also have to learn to work in groups and know how to organize.    

Meeting with the fourth year

        

The meeting with the 4th grade we liked a lot but we found it very long. We learned a lot           on how to decrease salt, fats etc. and most of us served a lot although it was difficult to take           All the notes. We were able to apply it in the recipes but in some it made us confuse a           little bit. Those who explained us were very well understood.

Logo

 

Our team's logo says “I-food” and has a chef's hat because we thought it meant    Something fun but also serious. This logo combines very well with our slogan.  Our slogan says "Food is important." Because we want them to know that we care that everyone eats well.  We chose something not so elegant because it is a catering for a school canteen.

Recipe selection

     

To choose the menus we take a      Little because we wanted to have food for different types of people but that are not similar, such as:      Vegans and vegetarians, but in the end we made it. We have very varied menus and although they are not very economical    They are perfect for every type of person. These are for hypertensive people, for celiacs and for people without any problem   We chose a very rich menu that we call international because it has a variety of foods from different countries. To achieve this, the 4th boys helped us in the meeting

Video tutorial

     

For the video tutorial we chose the nachos with guacamole dish and organized a day at the school. We decide when, where and how to do it.        We did it at the home of Albertina Z. on Sunday 3/11. We take pictures of the stages        of the preparation, we recorded a voiceover in English and put subtitles in Spanish.         To edit it we use the Video Maker application. First we put the ingredients together and started cooking.         While we were taking pictures we were preparing the dish. When we finish it, we edit it. We collaborate: Valentina J.,          Albertina Z., Martin J. and Joaquín G. (The house, the lemon, the tomato, the avocados, the salt and the pepper and the cilantro          of Albertina, Valentina's cell phone, Martin's nachos and Joaquin's onion).

Description of calculation of costs and final prices

     

Three members of the group made two spreadsheets, and the fourth made three. We did it on 12/11 using Excel.         Excel was shared with all members of the group. Each chose a recipe, we wrote the ingredients          in the Excel and we search in Mercado Libre, Carrefour digital or Coto digital the prices of the ingredient and write it in          the board. We had a meeting with the 6th year boys in which they helped us calculate the plate's profit.
