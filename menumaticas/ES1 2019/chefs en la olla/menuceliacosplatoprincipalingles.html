Ingredients

      
            
  • 500 grs celiac apt premix
    •         
  • salt 1 tablespoon
    •         
  • sugar 1 teaspoon
    •         
  • ten grams of dry yeast (one packet)
    •         
  • 1 ½ teaspoon of xanthica gum
    •         
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    •       

Procedimiento

Put all the dry ingredients in the processor. Then add olive oil and warm water. Mix until integrated (3 minutes approx.). Grease pizza molds and with the help of a spatula place the dough and spread it over the entire surface. Do not raise in a warm place until it doubles its volume (30 minutes approx.). Take the oven to 200 degrees and place the pizzera on the base of the oven, to brown it from below. Remove from the oven and place the tomato, the grated mozzarella. Then return to the oven now on the top grid. Remove from the oven when the cheese is melted, sprinkle the pizza without tacc with fresh oregano and chopped olives.
