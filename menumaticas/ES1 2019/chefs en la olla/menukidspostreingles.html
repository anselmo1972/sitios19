Ingredients

      
          
  • 2 cups of evaporated milk
    •       
  • 2 cups of milk cream
    •       
  • 1 cup of sugar
    •       
  • 1 tablespoon of extract (the flavor you like most)
    •       
  • 8 egg whites
    •       
  • 1 pinch of salt
    •       

Prosses

Beat the egg whites with the help of a wire whisk or electric mixer for a few seconds and then add the pinch of salt and the extract. Continue beating until the mixture is pastel yellow and incorporates the cream, also known as cream, and evaporated milk. Beat for a few minutes until the ice cream preparation thickens. Pour the mixture into a plastic container and place it in the freezer. Stir the preparation every 45 minutes so that it freezes slowly and becomes much softer and creamier. You should probably perform this procedure about 5 times so that it is suitable for consumption. But, if you want it much more consistent and with a texture similar to those bought at the ice cream shop, freeze it overnight. At the end of the rest period, remove it and serve in bowls with caramel, chocolate, strawberry or dulce de leche sauce and if you are sweet tooth, accompany with a fruit salad.
