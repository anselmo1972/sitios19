Prosses

Find all the vegetables you can think of and more: onion, onion, scallion, leek, carrot, zucchini, boiled corn, celery, beet, green pepper, green, red, yellow etc. Cut into mini squares, chopped type. Sauté them in a pan until they are soft, Season with what you like best. On the other hand, mix the minced meat. If you can, choose a lean cut and ask the caregiver to pick it up. Mix the meat with one or two eggs, depending on the amount you are making (it's just to choose from). In this step, you can add vegetables and seeds. Prove that half or only a little more is minced meat, and the other half, mix cereals and vegetables. Season. Make the hamburgers: form a ball and then crush it with both hands. Make sure they are well cooked before cooking.
