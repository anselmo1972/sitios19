Process

      

         Salpimentamos chicken breast and grilled, reserve. We wash the lettuce, you can use the variety you like, but if you want to make the most typical use romaine lettuce. We dry it and chop finely. We put the lettuce in the dish or dish, over the breast cut into small pieces or in strips, some croutons of bread and some pieces of Parmesan cheese. Add a little sauce, mix and grate Parmesan cheese on top.
