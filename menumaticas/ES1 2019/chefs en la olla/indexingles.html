Chefs En La Olla

Hello, we are Agustin A.P., Agustina H., Antonia D.S. and Federico P. and we are Los Chefs en la Olla!
We are a catering company for school canteens. This project is done from Goethe School with the teacher Melina S. and the collaboration of Veronica G. in Buenos Aires, Argentina. We are programming our own website, creating all the necessary materials ourselves.


Mission

Our mission is to provide food to all schools in Argentina, to meet all the needs when it comes to eating all students and teachers. With a very good quality of food, at a low price.


Vision

Our vision is to be recognized in all schools as the national leader of school food sales, in Argentina. Offering all kinds of food for students and teachers.
