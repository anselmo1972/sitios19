Ingredients

      
  • 1kg of tomatoes
    •   
  • 2 red onions
    •   
  • 1 tied basil
    •   
  • 1 jar of tapenade
    •   
  • Salt
    •   
  • Pepper
    •   
  • Olive oil

Process

Wash the tomatoes and cut them into thin and even slices. Place them on each plate forming a circle. Add some basil leaves on top, onions and salt and pepper. Finally add a spoonful of tapenade to place.
