OUR PROJECT We are a group of five first-year students from Goethe College, who worked on the Menumatics project during 2019. To do this, we had to think of ourselves as a catering service for school canteens and propose three menus of three courses each: starter, main course and dessert. On these pages we programmed our website in HTML and CSS. We hope you like it :)

MISSION Our mission is to make the Molks Kitchen website and get everything done on time. We want to make a nice website with good recipes that are easy and rich. We want to open the web to please people.

VISION Our Vision is to make our customers happy and improve our website. The most important thing is that the menu is delicious, because we want people to give us their feedback. We want to make recipes on the website, which we did in a video. (On November 6, 2019 we made the video of the chocotorta).
